welcome To Mathare Hospital!

Our Mission

In an age where malpractice threatens the lives of our community, we aim to create a safe environment by emphasizing precautions, safety, and welfare of our patients. We do that by striving to lower medical errors, reduce readmission rates, and educate our patients on how to live a healthy lifestyle.

About Us

Mathare Hospital is a registered and licensed level 4 faith-based hospital, located in nairobi, off Red Hill Road. Our hospital was founded with an aim to provide quality, affordable and wholesome healthcare services to our clients. NAH offers a range of services including Outpatient and Inpatient care, 24 hours Accident and Emergency, Theatre, Cancer Center, ICU/HDU, and Specialty clinics.

Our Vision

To be an excellent Christian center for wholesome healthcare education and research.

Our Core Values

customer focus, team work, wholeness, excellence
